About Us

Sri Sukhmani Institute of Engineering & Technology is one of the oldest and most popular Institutions of Punjab. The institute is strategically located on Delhi-Chandigarh highway, just 10 minutes drive from Chandigarh. Established in 1998, SSIET has become a premium Educational Institute. The Institute, a venture of Sri Sukhmani Group of Institutions, caters to offer best education in the fields of Engineering, Computer Application & Management. SSIET has been imparting quality technical education with a focus on all aspects of individual development & to produce professionals who can serve society. SSIET boasts of quality infrastructure; it has all the facilities like well equipped library, classrooms, Wi-Fi, seminar halls, bank, gym , café, separate hostel for boys and girls, sports ground ;highly qualified faculty and staff, easy location, state –of- the art labs, training and Placement cell.
