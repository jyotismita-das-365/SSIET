Departments

Sri Sukhmani Institute of Engineering & Technology offers specialized departments with expert faculty, modern labs, and industry-aligned curriculum to prepare students for future challenges.

We have well-established departments, each equipped with advanced facilities and dedicated faculty members. Our goal is to provide quality education, research opportunities, and hands-on training to help students excel in their chosen fields.

Computer Science & Engineering

Duration: 4 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 Pass in Physics, Chemistry & Maths

B.Tech in Computer Science is a dynamic undergraduate program focused on the study of computer systems, software development, and computational theory. Students explore a wide range of topics including programming languages, algorithms, data structures, computer networks, and artificial intelligence. Through hands-on projects and internships, they gain practical experience in software development, cybersecurity, and data analysis.

Graduates of B.Tech in Computer Science are highly sought after in industries such as technology, finance, healthcare, and entertainment, with roles ranging from software engineer to data scientist.

Civil Engineering

Duration: 4 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 Pass in Physics, Chemistry & Maths

A Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Civil Engineering offers a comprehensive education in the planning, design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure and built environments. Students delve into subjects such as structural analysis, transportation engineering, geotechnical engineering, and environmental engineering.

Graduates of B.Tech in Civil Engineering find opportunities in various sectors including construction, transportation, urban planning, and environmental consulting. Further specialization through advanced degrees or certifications enhances career prospects and enables professionals to contribute to shaping resilient and sustainable communities.

Electrical Engineering

Duration: 4 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 Pass in Physics, Chemistry & Maths

B.Tech in Electrical Engineering is a specialized undergraduate program that delves into the study of electrical systems, power generation, and distribution. Students explore subjects such as circuit analysis, electromagnetism, power electronics, and control systems. Through hands-on projects and laboratory experiments, they develop skills in designing, testing, and optimizing electrical systems.

Graduates of B.Tech in Electrical Engineering find opportunities in diverse industries including power generation, renewable energy, telecommunications, and automation.
