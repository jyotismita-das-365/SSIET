B.Tech in Computer Science is a dynamic undergraduate program focused on the study of computer systems, software development, and computational theory. Students explore a wide range of topics including programming languages, algorithms, data structures, computer networks, and artificial intelligence. Through hands-on projects and internships, they gain practical experience in software development, cybersecurity, and data analysis.

Graduates of B.Tech in Computer Science are highly sought after in industries such as technology, finance, healthcare, and entertainment, with roles ranging from software engineer to data scientist.
