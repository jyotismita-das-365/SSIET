A Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Civil Engineering offers a comprehensive education in the planning, design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure and built environments. Students delve into subjects such as structural analysis, transportation engineering, geotechnical engineering, and environmental engineering.

Graduates of B.Tech in Civil Engineering find opportunities in various sectors including construction, transportation, urban planning, and environmental consulting. Further specialization through advanced degrees or certifications enhances career prospects and enables professionals to contribute to shaping resilient and sustainable communities.
