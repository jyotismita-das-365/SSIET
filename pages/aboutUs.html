About Us

Sri Sukhmani Institute of Engineering & Technology is one of the oldest and most popular Institutions in Punjab. The institute is strategically located on Delhi-Chandigarh highway, just 10 minutes drive from Chandigarh. Established in 1998, SSIET has become a premium educational Institute. The Institute, a venture of Sri Sukhmani Group of Institutions, caters to offer best education in the fields of Engineering, Computer Application & Management. SSIET has been imparting quality technical education with a focus on all aspects of individual development & to produce professionals who can serve society.

SSIET boasts of quality infrastructure; it has all the facilities like well equipped library, classrooms, Wi-Fi, seminar halls, bank, gym , café, separate hostel for boys and girls, sports ground ;highly qualified faculty and staff, easy location, state –of- the art labs, training and Placement cell.



The institute has lush green, pollution free campus on Chandigarh – Ambala highway at Dera Bassi. It boasts of aesthetically laid parks and well planned landscaping.The Institute is just 12 kms away from Chandigarh Airport. SSIET provides a commitment to promote quality technical education, professional exposure and word class training. It is the commitment which was translated into reality with the establishment of SSIET.

Easy Location (Centrally located from Chandigarh, Panchkula, Patiala, Ambala).

Placements and interaction with various top industries and companies.

Modern technique of education with interactive smart class rooms and tutorial rooms.

Functionally and aesthetically spread out, the architectural plan builds on providing an intellectual ambience and learning exposure. The class rooms are spacious, well ventilated and well fit with comfortable seating arrangement. Tutorial rooms provide effective close interaction with the tutor within small group of students.The Institute is founded on the philosophy of ‘Service before Self’. Every student is treated as an individual with distinctive learning inclination and unique ability. It helps the Indian students in giving an opportunity to get global education and be at par with students across the world. We, at SSSIET understand that training & placement are the most vital part of any professional organization. We have a full time in- house training and placement cell.

The cell provides personal career counseling, seminars, workshops throughout the year to make our students employable. SSIET students have done exceedingly well in procuring jobs as well doing industrial training in renowned companies for which they have received appreciation letters from the companies. Companies like Eclerx, Allengers Medical Systems, Megrisoft, Indian Army have become regular recruiters because of the excellent on job performance of SSIET students. SSIET also works on improving the employability of its students by giving them personality development sessions both from internal resources as well as external guest lectures. The institute has a record of 85-90% placement of its students since inception.
