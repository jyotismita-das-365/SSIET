History

Established in 1998, Sri Sukhmani Institute of Engineering & Technology (SSIET) has been a pioneer in delivering quality technical education in the region. Affiliated to I.K. Gujral Punjab Technical University, Jalandhar and approved by AICTE, New Delhi, the institute has consistently worked towards creating a dynamic learning environment that fosters innovation, creativity, and industry-readiness.

The institute boasts a lush green, pollution-free campus on Chandigarh–Ambala highway at Dera Bassi, with aesthetically laid parks and well-planned landscaping. The Institute is just 12 kms away from Chandigarh Airport.

Vision

"To be a leading center of excellence in engineering education, research, and innovation, dedicated to nurturing globally competent professionals with strong ethical values, technical expertise, and a commitment to contributing towards sustainable development and societal progress."

Mission

  • ● Provide Quality Education – Deliver outcome-based, industry-relevant technical education using innovative teaching-learning methods and modern infrastructure.
  • ● Foster Research & Innovation – Encourage creativity, critical thinking, and research that addresses real-world challenges.
  • ● Promote Ethical & Social Values – Instill professional ethics, integrity, and a sense of social responsibility among students.
  • ● Enhance Employability & Entrepreneurship – Equip students with skills, knowledge, and confidence to excel in careers or start entrepreneurial ventures.
  • ● Collaborate for Growth – Build strong partnerships with industry, academia, and research organizations for mutual progress.
