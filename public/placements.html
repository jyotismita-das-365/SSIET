Placements @ SSIET

Empowering Careers, Shaping Futures

Overview

The Placement Cell at Sri Sukhmani Institute of Engineering & Technology is dedicated to bridging the gap between academia and industry. Our robust placement program ensures that students are well-prepared to launch successful careers in leading organizations across the globe.

Objectives of the Placement Cell

  • Facilitate campus recruitment drives for students across all branches.
  • Enhance employability skills through training and workshops.
  • Build strong industry-institute relationships for internships and placements.
  • Guide students in career planning and higher education opportunities.

Placement Training Activities

  • Aptitude & Reasoning Sessions
  • Technical Skill Development
  • Mock Interviews & Group Discussions
  • Resume Building Workshops
  • Soft Skills & Communication Training
  • Industry Expert Talks & Seminars
