Campus Facilities

Experience Excellence at SSIET

At Sri Sukhmani Institute of Engineering & Technology, we provide world-class facilities to foster holistic development, innovation, and comfort for our students. Explore our vibrant campus and discover the resources that empower your academic and personal journey.

Library & Digital Learning
  • Extensive collection of engineering, management, and research books
  • Access to e-journals, online databases, and digital platforms
  • Spacious reading rooms & collaborative discussion zones
Labs & Workshops
  • State-of-the-art labs for all engineering branches
  • High-speed computer labs
  • Mechanical, Civil, and Electronics workshops for hands-on training
Classrooms & Seminar Halls
  • Smart classrooms with projectors & audio systems
  • Modern seminar halls for lectures, workshops, and events
Sports & Recreation
  • Indoor & outdoor sports (cricket, basketball, badminton, etc.)
  • Annual sports meet & inter-college tournaments
Medical & Health Care
  • On-campus medical room with first-aid facilities
  • Tie-ups with nearby hospitals for emergencies
Hostel
  • Separate hostels for boys and girls
  • Hygienic mess serving nutritious meals
  • 24x7 security & common recreation areas
Transportation
  • College buses covering major city routes
  • Safe & comfortable commuting for students
Green Campus & Sustainability
  • Eco-friendly campus with lush gardens & trees
  • Rainwater harvesting & solar panels
